My Learning Plan


learning plan describes how I will operate within the Dev Academy Foundations and Bootcamp Programme.


What is your long-term goal or career pathway?


I’m not totally sure but atm my long-term goal is to get a job as a Junior Web Developer and be able to work from home. A position where I’m able to progress.


A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning


Strength:

I think one of my strengths is identifying my critical voice. This is something I have worked on recently and had to learn to recognize as I have been very self-critical of my abilities/the way I learn which has held me back in the past.

I think this strength will help support me during my learning journey as self-doubt will be a big part of this course and something I will have to overcome and catch myself when I start comparing myself to others learning or being self-critical to myself.


Limitation:

I think one of my limitations is that sometimes I struggle to start a task, I think this can affect my learning because I put off my work as it may seem overwhelming or make me anxious that I’m not gonna be able to understand, causing me to get behind in my learning.

Another one is managing my time. I have a habit of working throughout the whole day where my only breaks are when I eat. So a lot of the time I feel like I haven't been very efficient with my learning even though I’ve put in the hours causing burnout. I should be breaking my day into focus times so I get a balance of enough rest and learning.

I have tried to implement time boxing like the handbook recommends as my time management in the past hasn’t been the best because I'm prone to distractions. So now I use a timer for when I want to focus without getting distracted to prevent my inefficient time management.


What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?


I think I want to develop my communication skills while working on group projects as I know I’m going to struggle with explaining what I am doing/what we should be doing. Also when I ask for help I want to be able to explain my situation better as I feel like this is going to be hard for me because I’m nervous about someone trying to help/explain things to me that I just don’t get yet from past challenges.
I also want to get better at balancing my time and learning things faster as I feel like I’m on the slower side.



A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme. This should include:
  • An explanation of how you will manage yourself to work productively and safely with other learners, Facilitators and industry/community representatives

  • What you will do when things are building up and starting to get to you


I think the best way to manage myself to work productively is to use a timer to break my day into certain focus and rest times to keep me productive and to go for at least 3 outdoor or treadmill walks a week to keep my body moving.

I think when things start building and start getting to me I just need to remember this is something I‘ve never done before and there will be other learners feeling overwhelmed and in the same situation as I am. I can write a list and break down everything I have to do into smaller tasks/in the best order so I don’t have to try to remember everything in my head.


A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way


I think the best way to get help in a timely way would be to first google the problem then if that doesn’t work make a thread in the discord on either the help/tech questions server or to my homegroup. I also can ask for help from my facilitators.



A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team


What I would expect from the Facilitation team would be understanding of everyone's situation and being there when we ask for help. Also to be clear with course expectations.


Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things. For example, you might have whānau commitments like school drop-offs. It's important to plan these in.


I would like to have the evenings to be able to spend time with my family or play games with my sister or watch a show. To catch up with my friends on either Friday, Saturday or Sunday.
